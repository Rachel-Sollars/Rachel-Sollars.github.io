Hedgehog Life

Controls

WASD = Move
SPACE = Drop leaf

I made this game as a self-imposed jam, where I had two hours from start to finish. The inspiration came when I asked my mother for a prompt and she asked me to make her a hedgehog game.

Santa and the Unicorns

Controls

WASD = Move

I made this game for the Yogscast Gamejam in 2019. In this game I wanted to experiment with using a single control scheme within different minigame contexts.

Kitty Catch

Controls

AS = Left and Right

I made this game for the 121st weekly gamejam, in 2019. The theme of this jam was 'Tacocat'. I chose not to go with something related to palindromes as I thought that most entries would choose this route.
